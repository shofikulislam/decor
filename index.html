Our Family, Serving Yours

Decor Construction is a local, family-owed roofing, restoration, and construction company serving Oklahoma and Arkansas. Since our beginnings in 1987, we set out to treat all of our customers like family by offering honest, compassionate, and friendly service. We’ll be there for you, day or night, and will be your construction partner throughout the entire project.

Over the years, we have earned the reputation of being one of the best roofing, restoration, and construction companies in the area. Our unreleting committment to quality workmanship has made us the go-to contractors for clients who are looking for an experienced construction team to get the job done right. We are fair and honest and here to help you repair and protect your home or business.
